GRAPHIC DESIGN

As the proud owner of TheDabCorner™ LLC. based in St. Louis, MO, I've built a successful career in graphic design, offering unique solutions to a wide range of clients in all industries. My passion for design sparked at age eleven, using Paint.NET and GIMP to craft YouTube thumbnails, and was deepened by my creativity playing Minecraft. I've since honed my skills in Adobe Creative Suite, and have worked with clients in the cannabis industry, general public for tattoo and sticker drafting, blueprinting, as well as science and technology sectors!

I am constantly seeking new opportunities to learn, grow, and collaborate with forward-thinking and inspiring clients. With a solid foundation in graphic design, chemistry, and an unwavering commitment to professionalism, I am eager to help my clients achieve their objectives and make a significant impact on their businesses.

CHEMISTRY

My journey in the world of chemistry and cannabis began at the age of 14 when I started self-studying organic chemistry and the cannabis plant. Despite graduating with an impressive 3.98 GPA, I chose not to pursue higher education at an institution, opting instead for self-directed learning that allowed me to explore my interests at my own pace.

One of my most notable accomplishments is the development of my novel alkene-based multi-step process for optimizing the nucleation of THCa and achieving high-quality crystal formation using solventless extracts (a.k.a Hash-Rosin Diamonds).

Driven by curiosity and an entrepreneurial spirit, I am dedicated to constantly improving my skills and understanding in graphic design, chemistry, and other areas of interest. In my free time, I stay up-to-date on the latest developments in technology and cannabis, participate in online communities, and enjoy reading about science and progress.
