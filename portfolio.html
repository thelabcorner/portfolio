E-Commerce Platform

Full-Stack Web Development

This comprehensive e-commerce solution was developed for a specialty retailer looking to expand their online presence. The platform features a fully responsive design, secure payment processing integration, customer account management, and a robust inventory system that synchronizes with their physical store locations.

Client Urban Outfitters
Timeline 3 months
Technologies React, Node.js, MongoDB
Role Lead Developer

Project Goals & Challenges

The main objective was to create a seamless shopping experience across all devices while ensuring secure transactions and efficient inventory management. One significant challenge was integrating with their legacy point-of-sale system, which required custom API development to ensure real-time inventory updates.

Solution & Results

By implementing a microservices architecture and developing a custom middleware layer, we successfully bridged their existing systems with the new platform. The result was a 45% increase in online sales within the first quarter and a significant reduction in inventory discrepancies.
