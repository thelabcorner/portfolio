DECARBOXULATOR™

Decarboxylation Calculator

The DECARBOXULATOR™ is an advanced online tool designed for precise cannabinoid decarboxylation calculations. It utilizes sophisticated algorithms to determine optimal time and temperature settings, ensuring maximum cannabinoid activation and efficiency in cannabis processing.

Research Focus

This project focuses on optimizing decarboxylation, a critical step in cannabis processing that converts non-psychoactive cannabinoids into active forms. Accurate decarboxylation is essential for producing potent and consistent medicinal cannabis products.

Key Features

  • Algorithm-driven time and temperature calculations.
  • User-friendly interface for easy input and output.
  • Enhances potency and consistency of cannabis extracts.

TERPTEK™

Solventless Crystallization Method

TERPTEK™ is a groundbreaking solventless crystallization method for THCa, utilizing cannabis-derived terpenes as crystallization catalysts. This multi-phased technique redefines industry standards by avoiding alkane solvents and focusing on the natural chemistry of cannabis compounds.

Research Focus

This research explores sustainable and efficient solventless extraction and crystallization techniques. TERPTEK™ aims to harness the natural solvent properties of terpenes, minimizing the environmental impact and maximizing the purity of cannabinoid crystals.

Methodology Highlights

  • Terpene-based solvent system derived from cannabis biomass.
  • Subatmospheric vapor-phase separation for terpenoid fraction refinement.
  • Ultrasonic and thermal modulation for enhanced crystal formation.
  • Focus on quantum chemistry of crystallization for molecular precision.

Collaboration is welcomed for empirical validation through x-ray diffraction. If you have expertise in X-ray diffractometry, please reach out!

CARBOXYL™ Crystal Suite

Crystallization Reactor

The CARBOXYL™ Crystal Suite is an innovative crystallization reactor designed to optimize cannabinoid crystallization processes. It employs advanced algorithms and controlled environmental profiles to achieve high purity, optimal crystal size, and maximum yield in cannabinoid isolation.

Research Focus

This project aims to develop advanced equipment for cannabinoid purification, focusing on crystallization as a key separation technique. CARBOXYL™ is designed to improve the efficiency and scalability of producing high-quality cannabinoid isolates for medicinal applications.

Reactor Features

  • Precise temperature and environmental controls.
  • Algorithm-driven process optimization profiles.
  • Scalable design for various production volumes.
  • Enhances crystal purity, size, and yield.

HASHAPP™

Solventless Extraction Database

HASHAPP™ is a comprehensive data management platform tailored for solventless resin cultivation and extraction facilities. It provides tools to organize, analyze, and visualize critical data, enabling users to optimize processes for enhanced efficiency, profitability, and product quality.

Research Focus

This project addresses the need for robust data management in cannabis extraction. HASHAPP™ aims to empower facilities with the data insights necessary to improve consistency, optimize yields, and ensure regulatory compliance in the rapidly evolving cannabis industry.

Platform Capabilities

  • Centralized database for cultivation and extraction data.
  • Advanced analytics and visualization tools.
  • Customizable dashboards and reporting features.
  • Supports data-driven decision-making for process optimization.
